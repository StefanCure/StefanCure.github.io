About Us

Malbert Tilers & Projects is a well registered company under the South African rules and regulations of company registration protocols.
We were established in 2005 – 06 – 15 in Johannesburg after a long period of practicals and higher professional guidance of African the Best Tilers. Since then we have been committed to serving our client’s needs in terms of good quality work and professional advices at all times.
We’re based in Johannesburg (Soweto) but we have managed to satisfy as many clients as we can, ranging from local, provincial and international.

Local: we have done renovations and new site tiling in private houses and new big site all over Johannesburg and surrounding areas such as springs (with john 0825631367), Selcot (with Angelo 0825695216), Boysens (lakeside with Mr. Aslam 0832351362) randberg, boskrein, Vereeniging, woodmid and many more with marlin builders.
Provincial: Eastern Cape (Jeffry’s Bay – Marlin Builders Angus- 0836081521).
International: Nigeria – Lagos and Abuja , Anambra. since 2009 Till to date.
